Brands

APOTHEKE

Founder Chrissy Fichtl’s passion for soap making, scent formulation and essential oils are the roots of what created APOTHEKE in 2011. Chrissy learned about the beauty of essential oils, starting with sourcing ingredients directly from farms and teaching herself the art of soap making in her own kitchen. Over time, she has self taught herself about fragrance blending.

In the summer of 2012, APOTHEKE opened their first factory, a 3,000 square foot site in Brooklyn, New York. Chrissy’s husband Sebastian Picasso took on the lead as production. He aided in maintaining the philosophy that high-quality and luxury goods can still be made steps from home by real people.

CALYAN WAX CO

Driven by the desire to give Americans a simple way to join the fight against trafficking, we set out to build a candle company that does just that. Since our humble beginnings in 2017, it's been amazing to see the impact that we have been able to make together!

Calyan Wax Co. is run by a small (but growing!) team in North Texas. We design, develop, and create every candle from scratch with an obsessive dedication to beauty, quality, and performance. The result? Nothing but the best candles on the market that we're proud to put our names on.

ARCHIPELAGO

Welcome to the world of Archipelago—where complex fragrance, warm candlelight, and botanical-based products make a house a home. Luxury is more than what meets the eye; it's about products that enhance our lives, and brand experience is everything. They believe that the art of living well is about choosing simple products, with simple ingredients, to help manage the simple moments in our lives.

Their story began, with a modest supply of essential oils and natural ingredients, in a small garage in Santa Monica. With a view of Catalina, they set out to recreate the unique smells and experiences that were found in other parts of the world. Soon friends, family, and the local community started taking notice of what was coming out of that little garage and their company – inspired and named for the island in the distance, was created. Twenty years later, they have long outgrown that garage, but the essential oils and botanical-based ingredients, which were so important to them in the beginning, are still at the heart of our their story.

Relax, draw a bath, and take in a simple moment—and experience The Art of Living Well.

A WILD SOAP BAR

A Wild Soap Bar family has been around since 1995, carefully handcrafting all natural, scratch made soaps featuring responsibly wildcrafted and organically grown botanicals that are safe for all living things and a joy to use. Through our ongoing partnership with 1% For The Planet, at least 1% of gross sales are donated each year to environmental non-profits that closely align with our mission such as Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center, United Plant Savers, and Western National Parks Association.

Thier premium bar soaps are handmade with certified organic vegetable oils and they are moisturizing, biodegradable, cruelty free, minimally packaged, and do not contain any synthetic fragrance oils or other harmful chemicals. The sustainable organic palm oil we use is from Colombia and it's Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil certified as well as Rainforest Alliance certified.
