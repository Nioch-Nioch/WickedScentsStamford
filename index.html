About Our Candle Shop

Our shop offers a wide selection of candles and gifts for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a relaxing aroma to fill your home or a unique gift for a loved one, we have something for everyone. Our candles are hand-poured using high-quality ingredients, and we offer a range of scents to suit any preference. Additionally, our gift selection includes thoughtful and unique items that are perfect for birthdays, weddings, holidays, and more. Come visit our store and discover the perfect candle or gift for yourself or someone special!

Brands

APOTHEKE

Founder Chrissy Fichtl’s passion for soap making, scent formulation and essential oils are the roots of what created APOTHEKE in 2011. Chrissy learned about the beauty of essential oils, starting with sourcing ingredients directly from farms and teaching herself the art of soap making in her own kitchen. Over time, she has self taught herself about fragrance blending.

In the summer of 2012, APOTHEKE opened their first factory, a 3,000 square foot site in Brooklyn, New York. Chrissy’s husband Sebastian Picasso took on the lead as production. He aided in maintaining the philosophy that high-quality and luxury goods can still be made steps from home by real people.

CALYAN WAX CO

Driven by the desire to give Americans a simple way to join the fight against trafficking, we set out to build a candle company that does just that. Since our humble beginnings in 2017, it's been amazing to see the impact that we have been able to make together!

Calyan Wax Co. is run by a small (but growing!) team in North Texas. We design, develop, and create every candle from scratch with an obsessive dedication to beauty, quality, and performance. The result? Nothing but the best candles on the market that we're proud to put our names on.
